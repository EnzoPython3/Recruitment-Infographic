Recruitment Process Overhaul

A 6-Week Game Plan for Hiring Excellence

The Crisis: A Revolving Door

Our recruitment process, optimized for speed, has led to a critical business problem: a staggering number of new hires are leaving almost immediately. This isn't just a leak; it's a flood that costs us time, money, and morale.

The New Mission: From Volume to Value

To solve our attrition crisis, we are fundamentally shifting our definition of success. We are moving away from celebrating speed and focusing on what truly matters: hiring quality candidates who stay, grow, and contribute to our culture.

The Old KPI (Discontinued)

This metric incentivized speed above all else, leading to poor-quality hires and high turnover.

Our New North Star Metric

This metric aligns our team's goals with the company's need for stable, engaged, and long-term employees.

The 6-Week Implementation Timeline

Phase 0: Foundation & Kickoff (This Week)

  • Conduct "State of Recruitment" meeting to align the team.
  • Officially discontinue the old KPI system.
  • Form a "Process Tiger Team" to champion the change.

Sprint 1: Process Redesign (2 Weeks)

  • Rewrite all 6 job descriptions for radical transparency.
  • Create the new "Mutual Fit" 30-minute interview guide.
  • Define the mandatory hiring manager touchpoint process.
  • Build the 30-Day Retention Rate tracking sheet.

Sprint 2: Training & Pilot Program (2 Weeks)

  • Host a mandatory team training workshop with role-playing.
  • Launch the new process with 2 pilot roles to test and learn.
  • Conduct daily 15-minute check-ins to gather feedback.

Sprint 3: Full Rollout & Review (2 Weeks)

  • Analyze pilot learnings and refine the process.
  • Implement the new process across all roles.
  • Review the first retention data to measure early success.
